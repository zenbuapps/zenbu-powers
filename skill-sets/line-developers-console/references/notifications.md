# Console Notifications — Email & Notification Center

Source: `https://developers.line.biz/en/docs/line-developers-console/notification/`

The notification center of the
[LINE Developers Console](https://developers.line.biz/console/) delivers updates
in real time. This file lists the notification types, their triggering events,
and how to configure delivery.

## Table of contents

- Types of notifications that can be received
- Important announcements

## Types of notifications that can be received

| Type | Overview |
|---|---|
| **Important announcements** | Important announcements regarding the LINE Platform. |
| **Activity** | Your own activities on the LINE Developers Console. |
| **News** | Announcements from the LINE Developers site. |
| **Channel activity** | Activities related to channels for which you have the Admin role. |
| **Provider activity** | Activities related to providers for which you have the Admin role. |

---

## Important announcements

Notifies you of important announcements regarding the LINE Platform.

Example: "Notice Concerning Use of Information in Connection with Group
Restructuring (share target picker)".

> **Important announcements cannot be turned off.**

---

## Activity

Notifies you of **your own** activities on the console:

| Type of operations | Activity detail |
|---|---|
| Operations related to providers | Create a provider; Delete a provider; Send an invitation email to join a provider; Grant a provider role to your developer account. |
| Operations related to channels | Create a channel; Delete a channel; Send an invitation email to join a channel; Grant a channel role to your developer account. |

---

## News

Notifies you of announcements from the LINE Developers site. When
[news](https://developers.line.biz/en/news/) is published, the news title is
sent.

---

## Channel activity

Notifies you of activities related to channels for which you have the **Admin
role**:

| Type of channels | Activity detail |
|---|---|
| Channels in general | Delete a channel; Add a new member to a channel. |
| LINE MINI App channels | Channel status updates based on review; Enable search for the LINE MINI App; Automatically delete review files attached to the channel (when the review is complete, or if the review is not requested even after 30 days of upload). |

---

## Provider activity

Notifies you of activities related to providers for which you have the **Admin
role**:

- Delete a provider.
- Add a new member to a provider.

---

## Configuring notification types & delivery methods

Go to your profile in the LINE Developers Console. In the **Settings** section,
toggle the slider on (right) or off (left) next to a notification option to
enable or disable it.

> **Important announcements** cannot be turned off.

**Notification email** — To receive **email** notifications, the email address
registered in your console profile must be **verified**. If the profile shows
**"Your email is not yet verified"**, click **Get Verification Link** to verify
it. You receive email notifications only for the notification settings you have
enabled.

---

## Checking notifications

Click the **bell icon** in the top-right corner of the console to open the
notification center. A **green dot** next to the bell icon indicates unread
notifications. The notification center shows recent updates and activities.
